Golden Ratio Detector — Face / Building / Logo


Use face mode for portraits (automatic). Use Manual for buildings or logos.


How it works
Face mode loads a lightweight landmarks model (TensorFlow). It measures several facial distances and compares their ratios to φ. Manual lets you click two segments (A→B and C→D) and computes ratio A-B / C-D. Edges uses simple auto-suggestion but you should verify points.

Tips
For buildings & logos use Manual. Click two points for segment A, then two for segment B. Try to use visually consistent edges (e.g., full width vs full height).
